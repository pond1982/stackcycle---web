Coming to the App Store

A meditative tower-stacking game for iOS.

You wake on the Plinth without memory, given one knowledge: how to bind slabs. Tap to place each falling slab on the tower. Perfect alignment is the only act of grace — every misalignment is forever. Climb through eight atmospheric strata of the Upper Dark, each shifting the air around you. The tower will fall. The cycle continues.

"Slabs are the bones of dead universes. Each is a remnant of a tower raised long ago by a previous Anchorite — the role you now hold."

Strata of the Upper Dark

Eight biomes, each with hand-painted backdrops, ambient drones, and whispered voices of past Anchorites. As you climb, the air thins, the lighting shifts, and the cosmos around you starts to fail.

What's inside

One mechanic

Tap to bind. That's it. Slabs slide; you place them; the tower rises until it falls.

Eight strata

From The Settling to The Vault's Shadow — each shifts the atmosphere, lighting, ambient sound, and the whispers of past cycles.

Voices in the rubble

Misaligned binds scatter fragments that whisper in the voices of past Anchorites. Different situations summon different fragments.

Ship-shape

One-tap controls. Audio & haptics toggles. Closest-approach record across cycles. Designed for the silent commute.
